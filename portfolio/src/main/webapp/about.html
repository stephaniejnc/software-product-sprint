about me

Apart from spending hours on end debugging labs, cramming for exams, and being confined to the rainy campus, I enjoy hiking and skiing across the Rocky Mountains, fencing epee and sabre, as well as playing piano/flute/tuba! Feel free to drop me a message anytime! :)
